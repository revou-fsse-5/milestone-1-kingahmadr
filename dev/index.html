Gold Gym, a beacon of fitness excellence, has been transforming lives since its inception in 1965. Founded by Joe Gold in Venice Beach, California, Gold Gym quickly became synonymous with bodybuilding, strength training, and a dedication to peak physical performance.This humble establishment grew from a single location into an internationally renowned fitness brand, boasting over 700 locations in 28 countries.
